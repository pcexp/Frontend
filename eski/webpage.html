RAMBO FIRST BLOOD (1982)

First Blood is a 1982 American action film, the first film in the Rambo series.
Screenplay by Michael Kozoll, William Sackheim and Sylvester Stallone, based on the book First Blood by David Morrell. A war veteran Green Beret is forced by a tyrannical Sheriff and his deputies to flee into the mountains and wage an escalating personal war against his pursuers. The story begins with what happens to the protagonist, a brave Green Beret. The life of this veteran, who was unfairly accused by the sheriff and his men, changes completely. Targeted by the cruel Sheriff and his deputies, this veteran's only option is to escape to the mountains and survive.
